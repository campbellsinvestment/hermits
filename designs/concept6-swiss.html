About Us

A legacy of brotherhood, leadership, and service

Our Story

The Canada Hall Hermits represents a distinguished tradition of brotherhood at the University of the West Indies, St. Augustine campus. What began as a residence hall community has evolved into a global network of accomplished alumni united by shared values and lifelong bonds.

Since our founding in 1963, we have remained committed to excellence in academics, leadership in our communities, and service to those in need. Our members span continents and professions, yet we remain connected through our shared experience and dedication to our founding principles.

Our Mission

To foster lifelong connections among Canada Hall alumni, support current and future students through scholarships and mentorship, give back to our communities through service initiatives, and uphold the values that define us: integrity, excellence, service, and brotherhood.
