Canada Hall Legacy

The Canada Hall student residence was a gift from the Canadian Government in 1963 to the University of the West Indies. Historically accommodating male students, the hall now embraces co-education, welcoming both male and female residents in a vibrant community of scholars.

Purpose

OUR MISSION

To advance education or the development of student careers including the institution of apprenticeship programs, in-service training, career guidance, and other programs by providing publicly available scholarships, bursaries, and other forms of financial assistance to residents of the hall tenable at the University of the West Indies, St. Augustine campus.

Vision

LOOKING FORWARD

Hermits1963 Inc. vision is to provide sustainable financial support to needy residents of Canada Hall to assist in the completion of their course of study at the University of the West Indies, St. Augustine campus.

STRATEGIC PARTNERSHIP

Works with The Hermit Foundation (THF), a non-profit registered in Delaware, USA.
