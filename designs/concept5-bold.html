OUR BROTHERHOOD

More than an association - a lifelong bond

01

OUR STORY

Founded on the principles of brotherhood, leadership, and academic excellence, the Canada Hall Hermits represent decades of tradition at the University of the West Indies St. Augustine campus. What began as a residence hall community has evolved into a powerful network of accomplished alumni spanning the globe.

02

OUR MISSION

To foster lifelong connections among Canada Hall alumni, support current and future students, give back to our communities, and uphold the values that define us: integrity, excellence, service, and brotherhood. We believe in paying forward the opportunities we received.

03

OUR IMPACT

Through scholarships, mentorship programs, community service initiatives, and emergency relief efforts, Hermits around the world are making a tangible difference. From supporting education to disaster relief, our brotherhood stands ready to serve.
