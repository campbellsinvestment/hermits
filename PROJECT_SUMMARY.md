## ğŸ¨ Design Concepts Created

### 1. Heritage Classic (concept1-heritage.html)
**Theme**: Traditional, prestigious, timeless  
**Colors**: Burgundy (#800020), Gold (#FFD700), Navy, Cream  
**Typography**: Playfair Display, Crimson Text (Serif fonts)  
**Best For**: Formal presentation, emphasizing legacy and prestige

**Key Features**:
- University-style crest and insignia
- Formal hero section with elegant gradients
- Mission statement with decorative elements
- Traditional product showcase
- Academic color palette
- Stats highlighting 60+ year history

---

### 2. Modern Alumni Hub (concept2-modern.html)
**Theme**: Contemporary, engaging, user-friendly  
**Colors**: Blue (#2563eb), Light Blue (#dbeafe), Green (#10b981)  
**Typography**: Poppins, Inter (Modern sans-serif)  
**Best For**: Community engagement, mobile users, modern appeal

**Key Features**:
- Sticky navigation with CTA
- Card-based layouts
- Impact section with color-coded cards
- Event calendar with date badges
- Modern product grid with ratings
- Newsletter signup
- Glass-morphism effects

---

### 3. Dynamic Storytelling (concept3-storytelling.html)
**Theme**: Immersive, media-rich, cinematic  
**Colors**: Dark (#0f0f0f), Gold (#FFD700) accents  
**Typography**: Merriweather, Montserrat (Mixed serif/sans)  
**Best For**: Visual storytelling, media showcase, emotional connection

**Key Features**:
- Fullscreen video hero
- Interactive 60-year timeline
- Masonry photo gallery
- Video interview section
- Impact stories with testimonials
- Dark theme for media focus
- Parallax-ready design
