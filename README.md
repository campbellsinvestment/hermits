## ğŸ“ About Canada Hall Hermits

Canada Hall Hermits is a fraternity of past students from **Canada Hall** at the **University of the West Indies, St. Augustine** campus. 

**Founded:** 1963  
**Mission:** United in brotherhood, dedicated to excellence, committed to giving back  
**Focus:** Alumni engagement, student support, community building

For 60+ years, the Hermits have maintained strong bonds among alumni and supported current students through bursary programs, ensuring that financial barriers never stand in the way of academic excellence.
