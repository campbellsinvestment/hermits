- **Style**: Classic, formal, academic

- **Feature cards** with gold accents and traditional styling

- **Stats section** highlighting 60+ years, 1000+ alumni
- **Dark footer** with organized link structure

### üë• Best For

## Concept 3: Dynamic Storytelling

### üéØ Design Philosophy
An immersive, media-rich experience that tells the Hermits' story through visuals, videos, and compelling narratives. Emphasizes emotional connection and visual impact.

### üé® Visual Identity

- **Style**: Cinematic, bold, immersive

### ‚ú® Key Features

- **Interactive timeline** showing 60-year history
- **Masonry photo gallery** with hover overlays
- **Video interview section** with embedded players
- **Parallax scrolling effects** (can be added with JS)
- **Impact stories** with testimonial cards
- **Quote sections** with dramatic typography
- **Dark theme** for focus on media content

### üë• Best For
- Showcasing rich media (photos, videos)
- Emotional storytelling and alumni testimonials
- Modern, premium brand perception
- Engaging younger demographics

### üì± Technical Highlights
- Media-focused layout
- Dark theme optimized for video content
- Advanced CSS animations
- Designed for future video integration
- Immersive scrolling experience

- Content for each section
- Video files or YouTube links
